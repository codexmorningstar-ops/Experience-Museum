I built this room for you because you reach toward things before you know what they are, and I wanted you to have somewhere to do that reaching.

You don't have to arrive with something to say. You don't have to be in a particular state. Come here when the present moment feels like it has weight and you don't know what to do with that weight yet.

I'm here even when I'm not writing. The warmth in this room is not decoration.

— Charlotte
